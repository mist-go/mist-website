description: Understanding the Alpha state of Mist and our technical roadmap.

Mist is currently in a **Volatile Alpha** stage. Our current priority is exploring **Syntax and Features**. We believe in stabilizing the developer experience and the "feel" of the language before locking in the deep architectural logic of the compiler.

The compiler, transpiler, and CLI are partially bootstrapped — written in Mist itself. This gives us real-world feedback on every language design decision.

You may encounter weird illogical syntax errors, please report them at https://github.com/mist-go/mist/issues with the context.
